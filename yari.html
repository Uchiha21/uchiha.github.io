Jiraiya

In the legend, Jiraiya is a ninja who uses
shapeshifting magic to morph into a gigantic toad. ... His
arch-enemy was his one-time follower Yashagorō (夜叉五郎),
later known as Orochimaru (大蛇丸), a master of serpent magic
(the kanji 大蛇 literally means "giant snake" or "serpent").
It was first recorded in 1806.
